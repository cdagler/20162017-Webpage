IB Math SL - Y1

The IB DP mathematics standard level (SL) coursefocuses on introducing important mathematical concepts through the development of mathematical techniques. The intention is to introduce students to these concepts in a comprehensible and coherent way.

Intro to Computer Programming

This course provides an introduction to the analysis, design, and implementation of software solutions to simple problems. Topics covered include control flow, iteration, function, file I/O, searching and sorting. Other topics covered include numbering systems and binary arithmetic.

Robotics

This course introduces students to the working principles and foundational knowledge of robotics. Robots are used as platforms to engage students in both personalized and collaborative learning computing, science, technology, engineering, and math concepts.
